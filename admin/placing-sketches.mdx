---
title: "Pin each unplaced sketch to its real-world location"
description: "Use the Place Sketches tab to click a map location for each unplaced sketch. Each confirmation auto-saves coordinates to data.json on the local server."
---

The **Place Sketches** tab walks you through every sketch that hasn't been assigned a location yet. Each session picks up where you left off — sketches you skip reappear the next time you open the admin panel. Each time you confirm a location, the change is auto-saved to `data.json` on the server immediately.

<Tip>
  Use the search box in the top-right corner of the map to jump directly to a city or address. This is faster than scrolling when you know where a sketch was drawn.
</Tip>

## Place a sketch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the admin panel">
    Navigate to [http://localhost:3000/admin](http://localhost:3000/admin) while `npm start` is running. The **Place Sketches** tab is active by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the current sketch">
    The top bar shows the current unplaced sketch image along with its filename and a progress indicator reading **X of Y remaining**. This tells you how many sketches still need a location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the location on the map">
    Use the search box in the top-right to jump to a city or address, or pan and zoom the map manually to find where the sketch was drawn.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to drop a pin">
    Click anywhere on the map to place a temporary marker at that position. You can click again to move the marker before confirming.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the location">
    Click **Confirm Location** to save the coordinates for the current sketch. The marker becomes permanent on the map and `data.json` is updated automatically on the server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate between sketches">
    Click **← Back** to undo the previous placement and re-place that sketch, or click **Skip →** to defer the current sketch and move on. Skipped sketches reappear the next time you open the admin panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish the session">
    Continue placing or skipping sketches. When every unplaced sketch has been handled, the admin panel shows a completion message and reveals a **Save All** button as a final confirmation. Click it to flush the complete dataset to `data.json`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Every **Confirm Location** click auto-saves to `data.json` immediately via `POST /api/save-data`. The **Save All** button only appears once all unplaced images have been processed, as a final checkpoint.
</Note>
